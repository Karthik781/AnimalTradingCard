German Shepherd

The Thirteen Club is a club for senior German Shepherds of 12 years and older. Pet parents of senior purebred German Shepherds can join and receive a certificate, as well as find online sources and information about the GSD.

German Shepherds have a two-layer coat which is close and dense with a thick undercoat. The coat is accepted in two variants; medium and long. The long-hair gene is recessive, making the long-hair variety rarer. Treatment of the long-hair variation differs across standards; they are accepted but not competed with standard coated dogs under the German and UK Kennel Clubs
